Privacy Policy

TL;DR: We collect nothing and share nothing, except kinda sorta with Google

Log Files

Every website stores log files, and we’re no different. Log files contain information about the type of browser you use, access times, pages viewed, your IP address, and the page you visited before navigating to our website. We only use logs for debugging purposes, never for marketing activities. Log files are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Logs will never be shared with third parties, aside from our content delivery network, hosting provider, and log management vendor. All of these vendors have strict data confidentiality policies.

Google Analytics

InternetingIsHard.com uses Google Analytics to analyze how users interact with our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to store information about your browser, device, location, access times, pageviews, repeat visits, how you navigate our website, etc. This information is used to see which pages are popular, count visits, and improve your experience.

Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory. We only use first-party cookies on our website. First-party cookies are associated directly with the InternetingIsHard.com domain and are not tracked when you navigate away from our website. We never use third-party cookies to track your activity outside of InternetingIsHard.com, because that’s creepy.

This policy does not apply to any information collected offline or via channels other than this website. We don’t have a policy for that kind of stuff, because we don’t do much of anything offline.
